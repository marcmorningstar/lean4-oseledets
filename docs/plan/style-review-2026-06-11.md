## Dimension: naming

**Verdict:** A Mathlib maintainer would find the core 85% of names surprisingly idiomatic and merge-ready, but would block the PR series on the 22 hypothesis-label theorem names, the Ruelle13/ExteriorNorm/Fischer top-level namespace pollution, and the UpperCamelCase flag defs — all mechanical renames confined to the assembly layer.

**Summary:** The library splits cleanly into two naming regimes. The infrastructure layer — Cocycle/*, Ergodic/Birkhoff, MaximalErgodic, Kingman's public API, OseledetsLimit's spectral lemmas, ExteriorNorm's lemma names — follows Mathlib's grammar remarkably well: compositional snake_case theorem names (setIntegral_birkhoffSum_pos_nonneg_of_measurable, eigenvalues₀_cfc_of_monotoneOn), correct lowerCamelCase data defs (bandProjector, lamSing) vs UpperCamelCase Prop predicates (IntegrableLogNorm, TopGapMassEnvelope, IsSubadditiveCocycle), textbook hypothesis names (hT, hAmeas, hint'), idiomatic Greek binders, no unicode abuse, and even deliberate Mathlib-style namespace extensions (LinearMap.singularValues_le_opNorm marked upstreamable). The deviations are concentrated and systematic rather than diffuse: (1) 22 public theorems in the assembly/wiring files are named after the hypothesis slot they discharge or an internal milestone code (hub_of_growthFunction, hbridge_of_forward_graded, hspec_standing, forward_graded_overlap', perStratum_strongInduction, _of_S4) — a Mathlib reviewer would require every one renamed to spell its conclusion; (2) ~170 declarations (20%) live in generic or paper-label top-level namespaces outside Oseledets (ExteriorNorm, Frames, Fischer, ChainCore, Weyl, and the indefensible Ruelle13 named after a paper's Proposition 1.3), including a dot-notation misfire where Fischer.PosSemidef.det_le_det_of_le can never resolve via dot notation; (3) a handful of UpperCamelCase data defs (Vflag, Vslow, Vassembled, Sprod, and the triple-violation V') plus the milestone def L7_statement; and (4) headline results whose person-names parse wrong under the grammar (tendsto_kingman, furstenbergKesten_top where _top does not mean ⊤). Privacy hygiene quarantines most of the ugliness: the htg*/bcoc-style working names are private, only 3 _aux lemmas exist, and the rename burden for upstreaming is mechanical and concentrated in MET-specific glue files rather than the upstreamable mathematics.
